Our Economy

Currency

Our society uses Techno Coin, a cryptocurrency.

Whats a cryptocurrency?

Crypto-Currency is an online currency where it uses a security system called Cryptography to keep your money extremely safe. Due to the powerful protection, counterfeit is much more unlikely to happen.

Payment Sytem

I our community, wallets are thin credit card sized cards with a 2cm bulge in the middle for a camera. If you want to pay for anything, online or instore, take the super easy steps pushing the red button on the side, looking right into the camera, once the light on the top flashes green, put it one your payment device and you are done. This card is Provided free by the governement. All stores a nave a backup cards.

With this, we can incredibly reduce the amount of material we use like paper and factories that we would otherwise use for paper cash.

Trade

We trade a lot with other communities for goods. Using our currency and the protection on it allows us to trade without revealing much of our information to the buyer/seller. All trade must follow our law on trade. For trade a license is required. If your career involves trade, which most do, we supply you with the license at no cost. We mostly trade locally but sometimes internationally. We only trade with associations that comply with our standards.

Our Trade Standards

  1. Seller/Buyer must have no history of harm to the environment and its species or corruption of any sort.
  2. The seller/buyer must have good history with environment and also be reputable. After the Seller/Buyer has meet these standards, they must comply with our import/export laws.
